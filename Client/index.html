    📄 Terms & Privacy Policy

    Last updated: March 2025

    Contents
    1. Introduction 2. Data We Collect 3. How We Use Data 4. Data Storage 5. Your Rights 6. Contact Us

    1. Introduction

    CyberShield Vendor Hygiene Assessment Platform ("the Platform") is designed to help organizations evaluate their cybersecurity awareness and practices. By using this Platform, you agree to these Terms of Service and our Privacy Policy.

    This Platform is operated for demonstration and assessment purposes. All assessment data is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to any external server.

    2. Data We Collect

    The Platform collects and stores the following data locally in your browser only:

    • Display name and email address (as entered by you)
    • Assessment responses and scores
    • Assessment history and timestamps
    • Earned badges and preferences
    ⚠ No data is ever sent to any external server. All data lives in your browser's localStorage and can be deleted at any time.

    3. How We Use Data

    Data collected is used solely to:

    • Display your assessment scores and risk rankings
    • Track your progress over time
    • Generate personalized recommendations
    • Award badges and maintain leaderboard standings

    4. Data Storage & Security

    All data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This means:

    • Data is device-specific and browser-specific
    • Clearing browser data will remove all stored assessment records
    • No cookies are used for tracking
    • No third-party analytics or advertising services are employed

    5. Your Rights

    You have the right to:

    • Access all data stored by opening your browser's Developer Tools → Application → localStorage
    • Delete all stored data at any time by clearing browser storage or using the Reset button in your Profile settings
    • Export your data at any time using the CSV export feature

    6. Contact Us

    For questions regarding these terms or your data, contact the CyberShield platform administrator at [email protected].

    By continuing to use this Platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood these Terms and Privacy Policy.
